Omelette

Description

Omelettes are a simple to learn and satisfying breakfast food that will start your day off the right way.

Try your hand at this delicious breakfast staple!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Whip eggs up in a bowl
  2. Add a bit of milk
  3. Use butter to line pan
  4. Cook for 4 to 6 minutes
  5. Serve
