Hard Boiled Eggs

Description

Hard boiled eggs are a quick and easy snack to keep you full and your stomach placid.

Try your hand at this delicious breakfast staple!

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Insert eggs
  3. Boil for 6-9 minutes
  4. Crack and peel eggs
  5. Serve
