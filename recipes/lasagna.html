Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a popular dish served in many households. Many families have enjoyed this simple and easy to make dish.

Try your hand at this delicious dinner staple.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Shredded Mozzarella
  • Ground Beef

Steps

  1. Cook ground beef
  2. Prep lasagna into layers
  3. Bake for 45 minutes
  4. Serve
